F.A.Q Vault.

Is it possible to ship outside of Tbilisi?

Yes, we ship all around Georgia. We ensure your high-fidelity media is packaged in secure, anti-static industrial packaging and delivered safely to your exact location.

What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs depend on your location within Georgia: Tbilisi Zone – 7.99 GEL Outside of Tbilisi (e.g., Batumi, Kutaisi, Rustavi, and other cities) – 9.99 GEL Mountainous regions outside of Tbilisi – 10.99 GEL Shipping fees are calculated based on the delivery region and will be shown during checkout.

Are your discs original?

Absolutely. Every single unit in our vault is a 100% authentic, original pressing imported directly from verified distributors in the United States and Europe. We strictly guarantee zero bootlegs and zero counterfeit media.

Do you take custom requests?

Yes. If you are hunting for a specific master recording, limited edition, or out-of-print physical copy, reach out to us via Instagram. Our curators will track it down through our international network and provide a secure quote.

Are the CDs factory sealed?

Yes, all of our brand-new imports arrive completely factory sealed. We preserve the pristine condition of the packaging so you get the true, authentic unboxing experience.

What is the return policy?

Damaged or Defective Items: We will return the item and refund if the received CD is cracked, skips, has manufacturing defects or order has a missing item. Upon receipt, we will offer a replacement (if in stock) or a full refund. New/Sealed CDs Must be returned in their original, unopened shrink-wrap. Once the seal is broken or the plastic wrap is removed, we cannot accept a return unless the disc is found to be defective. Customer Error/Change of Mind: We will not return items for "ordered by mistake" or "no longer want.
