F.A.Q Vault.

Can you ship outside of Tbilisi?

Yes, we ship all around Georgia. Your order is packed securely and delivered straight to your address.

What are the shipping costs?

Tbilisi Zone – 7.99 GEL Outside Tbilisi (Batumi, Kutaisi, Rustavi, etc.) – 9.99 GEL Mountainous regions – 10.99 GEL

Are your CDs original?

Yes. Every CD we sell is a 100% authentic original pressing imported from the US or Europe. No bootlegs, no fakes.

Do you take custom requests?

Yes. If you are looking for a specific album, reach out to us on Instagram. We will find it and get back to you with a price.

Are the CDs factory sealed?

Yes, all our new imports arrive factory sealed so you get the full unboxing experience.

What is the return policy?

If your CD arrives cracked, skips, or has a manufacturing defect — we will replace it or give you a full refund. Sealed CDs can only be returned unopened. We do not accept returns for "ordered by mistake" or "changed my mind."
